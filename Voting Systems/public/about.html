An online voting system is a software platform designed to securely conduct votes. These systems are crucial for balancing ballot security, accessibility, and the overall requirements of a voting event.

Key features of online voting systems include:
- **Integrity Protection**: Prevents multiple votes by the same voter.
- **Eliminates Physical Voting**: No need for in-person voting, paper ballots, or insecure survey methods.
- **Real-Time Results**: Allows for immediate scrutiny of results.

Often referred to as e-voting or electronic voting, these systems are a secure solution for gathering input and managing elections efficiently.

**Online Voting**: The safest way to conduct small-scale elections, especially during the COVID-19 pandemic.
